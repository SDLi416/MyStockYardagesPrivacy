Privacy Policy for My Stock Yardages

Last Updated: June 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how My Stock Yardages ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information Collection

My Stock Yardages does not collect any personal information from its users. The App:

  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not collect any personal data
  • Does not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not connect to the internet
  • Does not use analytics tools
  • Does not display third-party content or advertisements

Data Storage

Any data created while using the App is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data, nor is it transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

My Stock Yardages does not utilize any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect user data.

In-App Purchases

The App does not offer any in-app purchases or subscription services.

Children's Privacy

My Stock Yardages does not collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
