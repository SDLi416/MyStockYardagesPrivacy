English

This policy explains how NoteLoom handles information when you capture learning material, create notes, use Shortcuts, or purchase Lifetime Pro.

On-device processing and storage

NoteLoom does not require an account. Screenshots are normalized, text is recognized with Apple Vision, and summaries are generated locally with Apple Foundation Models when available or a local extractive fallback. Screenshots, OCR text, summaries, folders, and notes stay in the app's local storage and are not sent to NoteLoom or third-party servers.

Lifetime Pro purchases

Apple processes the non-consumable Lifetime Pro purchase through StoreKit. NoteLoom reads Apple's verified transaction on the device to determine whether Pro is unlocked. The app does not send transaction records, purchase history, or Apple Account information to a developer server. Apple handles payment and refund information under its own terms.

Tracking, advertising, analytics, and sharing

The current App Store build does not transmit screenshots, OCR text, summaries, folders, usage records, or purchase records to NoteLoom or third-party servers. It contains no advertising or third-party analytics SDK, performs no cross-app tracking, and does not sell personal information.

Retention and deletion

You can delete individual notes and folders or delete all local notes from Settings. Uninstalling NoteLoom removes its local app data unless a copy remains in a device backup managed by Apple. The included-summary count is stored locally and may reset when the app is deleted; a purchased Pro entitlement can be restored through Apple.

Children, changes, and contact

NoteLoom is intended for general study use and does not knowingly collect children's personal information. Only process content you have the right to use and avoid retaining unnecessary sensitive information in screenshots. We may update this policy as features change and will publish the revision date here.

Privacy questions or requests: icubic3@gmail.com
