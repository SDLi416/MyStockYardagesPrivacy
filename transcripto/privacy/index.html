English

This policy explains how Transcripto handles information when you import media, transcribe speech, translate text, generate narration, and manage local tasks.

Information processed on your device

Transcripto processes audio or video you choose to import, generated transcripts and translations, bilingual documents, narration audio, task names and status, favorites, language choices, voice choices, and notification preferences. These items are stored in the app sandbox or its App Group container.

No developer data collection

The developer does not collect, track, sell, or upload your imported media, generated content, task records, or preferences. Transcripto requires no account and contains no advertising, analytics SDK, or third-party AI service.

Apple system services

Transcripto uses Apple frameworks for on-device speech recognition, translation, speech synthesis, notifications, and file access. When you prepare a language for the first time, the operating system may contact Apple to download a speech model or translation resource. Apple manages that process under its own privacy policy.

Purchases

If you purchase Transcripto Pro, Apple App Store and StoreKit process the payment, transaction verification, and purchase restoration. The developer does not receive your complete payment details or send purchase data to a developer server.

Storage, export, and deletion

Your data remains local unless you deliberately export or share it through an Apple system sheet. Delete an individual task to remove its associated local files, use Settings to clear local tasks, or delete Transcripto to remove its app container. Copies may remain in locations you exported to or in backups managed by Apple.

Permissions

Speech recognition is used only to transcribe media you select. Notifications are optional and only report local processing completion. File access occurs through Apple pickers or share actions that you initiate. You can change permissions in system Settings.

Children, changes, and contact

Transcripto is not designed to collect personal information from children and provides no account, social, chat, or public-posting features. We may update this policy if the app's data practices change and will publish the revision date here.

Privacy questions: icubic3@gmail.com
