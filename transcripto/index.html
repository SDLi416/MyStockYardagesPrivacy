Transcripto

Transcribe, translate, and listen on your device.

Import audio or video, create a transcript, translate it, and generate spoken audio with Apple on-device technologies. No Transcripto account or developer backend is required.

Privacy Policy Support & Data Deletion

Local by design

Imported media, transcripts, translations, and generated narration stay in Transcripto's local storage unless you choose to export them. Apple may download speech models and translation language resources the first time a language is prepared.

导入的媒体、转录文本、译文和朗读音频默认保存在本机。首次准备语言时，系统可能需要从 Apple 下载语音模型或翻译资源。

Requires iOS or iPadOS 26 or later. Available languages depend on the device, system version, and Apple resources.
