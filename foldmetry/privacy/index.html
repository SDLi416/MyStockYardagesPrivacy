Privacy Policy

Foldmetry is designed to train spatial reasoning without building a profile about you. The app works offline, requires no account, and does not transmit your training activity to us.

English

Short version: Foldmetry does not create an account, show ads, use analytics, track you across apps or websites, or send your exercise history to the developer. Progress and preferences stay on your device.

1. Scope

This policy explains how the Foldmetry iOS and iPadOS application (the “App”) and Foldmetry’s public support pages handle information. It applies to version 1.0 as described here. Apple’s App Store, Apple Account, StoreKit, email providers, and website-hosting services operate under their own privacy policies.

2. Information handled by the App

InformationHow it is handled
Training progress, answers, streaks, history, and settingsGenerated and stored locally on your device. The App does not upload this information to Foldmetry.
Account or profile informationNot requested. Foldmetry has no user-account system.
Advertising or analytics identifiersNot requested or used. The App contains no advertising or third-party analytics SDK.
Location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, health, or biometric dataNot requested or accessed by the App.
Purchase statusApple processes purchases. StoreKit provides signed product and entitlement information so the App can unlock Pro features. The App verifies it on device and does not send transaction data to a Foldmetry server.

3. Tracking and advertising

Foldmetry does not track you across apps or websites, create an advertising profile, sell personal information, or share app activity with data brokers or advertisers. The App does not request App Tracking Transparency permission because it does not engage in tracking.

4. Local storage and deletion

The App uses Apple-provided local storage to remember preferences, progress, and training history. This data remains under your device and backup settings. You can use the in-app reset option to clear training data where available, or delete the App to remove its local container from the device. A device backup may retain a copy according to your Apple backup settings; Foldmetry cannot access or delete that backup for you.

5. Purchases

Annual subscriptions and lifetime purchases are offered through Apple’s StoreKit. Apple—not Foldmetry—handles payment details, tax, billing, trial eligibility, refunds, and Apple Account authentication. Foldmetry receives no credit-card or bank details. Your use of the App Store is governed by Apple’s terms and privacy policy.

6. When you contact support

If you voluntarily email support, the email address, message, attachments, and technical details you choose to include are processed by the sender’s and recipient’s email providers. We use that correspondence only to investigate and answer your request, protect the service, or meet a legal obligation. Do not send passwords, full payment details, government identifiers, health information, or other unnecessary sensitive data.

Support correspondence is not collected by the App and is not used for advertising or sold. We keep it only as long as reasonably needed to resolve the request and maintain necessary business or legal records, then delete or de-identify it when practical. You may ask us to delete a support conversation, subject to applicable legal requirements.

7. Public website

The Foldmetry website is intended to be static and uses no behavioral analytics, advertising pixels, or cross-site tracking cookies. Its hosting provider may automatically process limited request data—such as an IP address, time, requested page, and browser information—to deliver pages, prevent abuse, and maintain security. Such infrastructure logs are controlled and retained according to the host’s configuration and policy; Foldmetry does not use them to profile App users.

8. Children

Foldmetry does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. The App’s exercises are educational and do not include social features or user-generated content. A parent or guardian should manage the child’s device, Apple Account, purchases, and support contact as appropriate.

9. Security and international processing

Keeping training data on device reduces exposure, but no device or electronic system is completely secure. Support email and website delivery may be processed in regions where their providers operate. Those providers are responsible for their own safeguards and legal transfer mechanisms.

10. Your choices and rights

Because Foldmetry does not receive your in-app training data, we generally cannot access, export, correct, or identify it. You control it on the device. For support correspondence that we can reasonably identify, you may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion where applicable law provides those rights. We may need enough information to verify and locate your request, but will not ask for unrelated information.

11. Changes

If the App’s data practices change—for example, if a future version introduces cloud sync, accounts, analytics, or a new service—we will update this policy and the App Store privacy disclosures before or when that change takes effect. Material changes will receive an updated effective date and appropriate notice.

12. Contact

Privacy or support questions: icubic3@icloud.com

Support page: Foldmetry Support

本政策说明 Foldmetry 折维 iOS 与 iPadOS 应用（以下简称“本应用”）以及公开支持页面如何处理信息，适用于此处描述的 1.0 版本。Apple 的 App Store、Apple 账户、StoreKit，以及邮件与网站托管服务由相应提供方依其隐私政策运营。

3. 追踪与广告

Foldmetry 不会跨应用或网站追踪你，不建立广告画像，不出售个人信息，也不会向数据经纪商或广告商分享应用内活动。本应用不进行追踪，因此不会请求“App 跟踪透明度”权限。

4. 本地存储与删除

本应用使用 Apple 提供的本地存储记录偏好、进度和训练历史。这些数据由你的设备与备份设置管理。你可以使用应用内的重置选项清除训练数据（如当前版本提供），也可以删除本应用以移除设备上的本地容器。设备备份可能会依你的 Apple 备份设置保留副本；Foldmetry 无法访问或代你删除该备份。

5. 购买

年度订阅与永久版购买通过 Apple StoreKit 提供。付款信息、税费、扣款、试用资格、退款与 Apple 账户验证均由 Apple 而非 Foldmetry 处理。Foldmetry 不会取得银行卡或信用卡资料。使用 App Store 时适用 Apple 的条款与隐私政策。

6. 主动联系支持时

如果你主动发送支持邮件，邮件地址、正文、附件，以及你自行提供的技术信息，会由收发双方的邮件服务商处理。我们仅用这些内容调查并回复请求、保护服务或履行法律义务。请勿发送密码、完整付款资料、政府证件号码、健康信息或其他不必要的敏感数据。

支持邮件并非由本应用收集，也不会用于广告或出售。我们仅在解决请求和保留必要业务或法律记录所需的合理期限内保存，之后会在可行时删除或去标识化。你可以要求删除支持对话，但法律要求另有规定的除外。

7. 公开网站

Foldmetry 网站计划采用静态页面，不使用行为分析、广告像素或跨站追踪 Cookie。网站托管服务商可能为传输页面、防止滥用与保障安全而自动处理少量请求信息，例如 IP 地址、访问时间、请求页面和浏览器信息。这类基础设施日志由托管配置与其政策管理；Foldmetry 不会用它建立应用用户画像。

8. 儿童

Foldmetry 不会有意收集任何人的个人信息，包括儿童。本应用提供教育练习，不含社交功能或用户生成内容。家长或监护人应视情况管理儿童的设备、Apple 账户、购买与支持联系。

9. 安全与跨境处理

把训练数据留在设备上可以减少暴露，但任何设备或电子系统都无法保证绝对安全。支持邮件与网站传输可能会在相关服务商运营的地区处理，其安全措施和合法传输机制由相应服务商负责。

10. 你的选择与权利

由于 Foldmetry 不会取得应用内训练数据，我们通常无法访问、导出、更正或识别这些数据；它由你在设备上控制。对于能够合理识别的支持邮件，在适用法律赋予相关权利时，你可以联系我们请求访问、更正或删除。我们可能需要足以核实并定位请求的信息，但不会要求无关资料。

11. 政策变更

如果应用的数据处理方式发生变化，例如未来新增云同步、账号、分析工具或新的服务，我们会在变更生效前或生效时更新本政策及 App Store 隐私披露。重大变更会标注新的生效日期，并以适当方式通知。
